Triple Chocolate Chunk

Ever wantd to make an entire households mouths water all at once? Making these cookies will have all of your kids in the kitchen, eager to help cook and do whatever chores they need to do to get just a single bite. Use this power at your own risk

This recipe is a very fast and simple one that you can bake in just 10 minutes after preparing them. Still great if made from frozen dough!

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Unsalted Butter
  • 1/2 Cup White Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1 1/2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 3/4 Teaspoon Bakind Soda
  • 1 Cup Milk Chocolate Chips
  • 1 Bar Dark Chocolate
  • 3 Tablespoons White Chocolate Chips

Steps:

  1. Combine brown sugar, butter white sugar and salt in large bowl. beat until creamy and dark brown. Add eggs and vanilla, beat until light and smooth
  2. Mix flour and baking soda in seperate bowl. Slowly add the dry to the wet, until mostly incorporated but some traces of flour remain. Fold in all chocolates using spatula. Cover and refigerate for 0.5-48 hours.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 F
  4. Divide dough into 2 1/4 ounce portions and place on cookie sheet
  5. Bake 10-12 minutes. Cool for 30 Minutes
