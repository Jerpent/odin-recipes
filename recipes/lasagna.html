Momma Guiseppis Lasagna

This lasagna is the exact way that momma used to make it. Impress anyone you feed with this simple elegant recipe that will take them back to Italian origins.

For this recipe, you will want to schedule out a sizeable amount of time, but every second you set aside will be well worth it.

Ingredients:

  • 0.5lb Ground Beef
  • 0.5 Jar Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1 Can Diced Tomato
  • 0.5 Onion, Chopped
  • 1 Clove Garlic, Minced
  • 2 Teaspoons Dried Basil
  • 2 Teaspoons dried Oregano
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 2 Cups Dried Mafalda Noodles
  • 1 Cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Steps:

  1. Heat skillet and brown beef. Drain grease. Add Spaghetti Sauce, Tomatoes, Garlic, Basil, Oregano, Salt, and Pepper. Cook over low heat until sauce is hot.
  2. While sauce is heating, fill a pot with lightly salted water and bring to rolling boil. cook noodles until tender yet firm (about 8 minutes) Drain.
  3. Add noodles to the sauce and stir thuroughly. Sprinkle cheese on top
  4. Preheat the ovens broiler with rack 6 inches below the heat source
  5. Place skillet on rack until cheese is golden and bubbly (3 to 5 minutes)
